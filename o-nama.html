Zašto smo mi Xi-jajni?

Why are we Xi-jajni (great)?


Kineski karakter 喜 (Xi, čita se "si") označava sreću, radost i uživanje. Mi naša jela pravimo sa ciljem da se vi osećate srećno uživajući u najlepšim ukusima koje Kina ima da ponudi. Imamo dugogodišnjeg iskustva u spremanju tradicionalnih i novih kineskih jela najvećeg kvaliteta. Koristimo originalne kineske sastojke i garantujemo da ćete se zaista osećati kao da ste na gastronomskom putovanju kroz kineske regije, dok istražujete bogatstvo ukusa i tekstura koje naša kuhinja nudi. Naša strast prema kvalitetu hrane odražava se u svakom zalogaju. Uz svaku porciju naših jela, pružamo vam iskustvo koje će vam probuditi sva čula i ostaviti vas s osmehom na licu. Dođite i uživajte u pravoj kineskoj gastronomiji, dobrodošli u restoran "Xi‑jajno"!

The Chinese character 喜 (Xi, pronounced "see, she") symbolizes happiness, joy, and enjoyment. We prepare our dishes with the aim of making you feel happy while enjoying the finest flavors that China has to offer. With years of experience in preparing traditional and new Chinese dishes of the highest quality, we use authentic Chinese ingredients and guarantee that you will truly feel like you are on a gastronomic journey through Chinese regions, exploring the richness of flavors and textures that our cuisine offers. Our passion for food quality is reflected in every bite. With each serving of our dishes, we provide you with an experience that will awaken all your senses and leave you with a smile on your face. Come and enjoy authentic Chinese cuisine, welcome to the "Xi-jajno" restaurant!

Our name is a pun in Serbian. Xi-jajni is read as "sjajni", which means "great, excellent" in Serbian.
